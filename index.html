About Us

Founded in 2015, Houston Website Design emerged from a vision to provide local businesses with world-class web solutions. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into Houston's leading web design agency, serving over 500 successful clients across Texas.

Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge digital solutions that drive growth and success. Our core values of excellence, integrity, and client success guide every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your comprehensive web design package?

Our all-inclusive package features custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, premium hosting, SSL certification, regular backups, and 24/7 support. We handle everything from domain setup to launch and maintenance.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, following our proven 5-phase process: Discovery, Design, Development, Testing, and Launch. Complex projects may require additional time, which we'll discuss during initial consultation.

What ongoing support and maintenance do you provide?

We provide comprehensive maintenance including security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency assistance, and we conduct monthly performance reviews.

How do you ensure websites are secure and protected?

We implement enterprise-level security measures including SSL certificates, regular security audits, malware scanning, firewall protection, and automated backups. Our hosting infrastructure is monitored 24/7 for any security threats.

What makes your designs different from other agencies?

Our designs are crafted based on extensive market research, user behavior analysis, and conversion optimization principles. We focus on creating unique, brand-aligned experiences that drive real business results, not just aesthetic appeal.
